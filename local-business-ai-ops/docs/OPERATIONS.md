# Operations Guide

## Weekly Routine
- Monday: Topic pool + approval
- Tuesday-Wednesday: Script package
- Thursday: Calendar + engagement templates
- Friday: KPI report + optimization plan

## KPI Definitions
- CTR: Click-through rate
- 3s Retention: First-3-second hold rate
- Completion Rate: Video completion percentage
- Leads: Qualified DM/comment inquiries
- Conversion: Leads to consultation/order

## Risk Control
- Avoid policy-violating promises
- Avoid fake engagement patterns
- Keep platform-specific compliance checklist
